Task Focused IR in the Era of Generative AI

September 28-29, 2023

The Workshop on Task-based Informationa Retirval

Scope and Topics

Task-based information retrieval in generative AI is a type of information retrieval that uses generative AI models to retrieve information that is relevant to a user's task. Generative AI models are able to generate new text, images, or other data that is similar to the data they have been trained on. This allows them to be used to retrieve information that is not explicitly stated in the user's query, but that is implied by the task that the user is trying to complete.

Task-based information retrieval in generative AI is a promising new approach to information retrieval. It has the potential to improve the accuracy and efficiency of information retrieval, and to make it easier for users to find the information they need.
